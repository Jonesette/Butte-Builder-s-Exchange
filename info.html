Plan Checkout

Plans may be checked out from the office and must be returned by 9:00 AM the following workday. Plans may not be checked out the day prior to the bid date.

Plans bay be dropped off or scheduled to be picked up prior to/after business hours, using our drop box.

Plans returned after 9:00 AM will recieve a warning for the first offense. A second offence will warrant no checking out of plans for three months; a third offence will result in the loss of checkout privleges for six months. DON'T BE LATE!!!!
